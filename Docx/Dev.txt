What is Data Sovereignty (in Microservices)?In microservice design, the Data Sovereignty Principle states that every individual service must completely own and isolate its own database layer. No other service is ever allowed to read or write directly to that database.🚨 The Broken Monolith Way (Violating Data Sovereignty):If your Blog Service needs to know a user's email, a junior developer might write a SQL query inside the Blog code that hooks directly into the Auth Service database tables.Why this breaks: If the Auth team decides to change a table column name from Email to UserEmail, your Blog Service instantly crashes.
